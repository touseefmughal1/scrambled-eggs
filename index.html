SCRAMBLED EGGS

Eggs are one of my favorite food. here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrabled eggs.

INGRIDIENTS

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

METHOD

  1. Melt butter in frying pan a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatola fold the eggs from edge of the pan to the center every 20 second(as if you are making an omellete)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
